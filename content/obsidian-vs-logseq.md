You're picking between [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md) and [Logseq](https://logseq.com). Both serve research workflows. Here's how they actually differ.

| | Obsidian | Logseq |
|---|---|---|

## Where Obsidian wins

- Files are plain markdown — no lock-in
- Strong plugin ecosystem
- Works offline

### Obsidian downsides

- Sync requires paid add-on or self-hosting
- Steep ramp on plugins

## Where Logseq wins

- 100% free + open source
- Outliner UX
- Privacy-first (local files)

### Logseq downsides

- Mobile app less polished
- Smaller plugin community than Obsidian

## Verdict

- **Pick [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md)** if you value: local-first markdown workflow, research that depends on backlinks.
- **Pick [Logseq](https://logseq.com)** if you value: outliner-style note-taking, open-source Roam alternatives.

If you're starting from zero, the free tier of either lets you ship your first project before you commit. That's the right way to choose.
