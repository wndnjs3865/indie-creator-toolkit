## FAQ

**Is there a truly free option for podcast editing software?**
Yes — Audacity is free forever.
For everything else, the free tier is enough to validate before paying.

**What's the cheapest paid option that's still serious?**
Auphonic at $13/mo.

**Do I need podcast editing software at all when starting out?**
Honestly, no. Most solo podcaster ship their first project with whatever's on their laptop already. Tools matter once friction starts costing you hours per week.
