You're picking between [Auphonic](https://auphonic.com) and [Descript](https://descript.com). They look similar (both clean up your podcast audio with AI) but they're fundamentally different tools. Auphonic is a *mastering* service — upload your edited file, get back a leveled, noise-reduced, podcast-ready version. Descript is a full *editor* — record, edit, master, transcribe, all in one app. Here's how to pick.

## How to choose

- **Pick [Auphonic](https://auphonic.com)** if you already have an editor you're comfortable with (Audacity, REAPER, GarageBand) and you only want a clean, automated mastering step. $13/mo for the free tier overflow + direct host upload is honest pricing for a focused tool.
- **Pick [Descript](https://descript.com)** if you want one tool for the entire pipeline — record, edit, master, transcribe. The text-based editing is a workflow upgrade vs Audacity + Auphonic combined.

## The math

If you only need mastering: Auphonic at $13/mo beats Descript at $16/mo by $3/mo.

If you need editor + mastering: Audacity (free) + Auphonic ($13/mo) totals $13/mo and requires juggling two tools. Descript at $16/mo gives you both in one workflow with text-based editing on top — net $3/mo more for substantially less friction.

The decision is whether you value tool focus (Auphonic + Audacity) or workflow consolidation (Descript).

## Verdict

| Situation | Pick |
|---|---|
| Already happy with your editor | **Auphonic** |
| Want one-tool solo workflow | **Descript** |
| Budget-tight, free tiers matter | **Auphonic** (2 hrs/mo) |
| Plan to add video / YouTube | **Descript** |
| Mastering-quality is the priority | **Auphonic** |
| Editing speed is the priority | **Descript** |
| Use Buzzsprout/Castos host | **Auphonic** (direct upload) |

If you're between the two — and you're a solo podcaster starting out — Descript's all-in-one workflow is probably the right call. The $3/mo premium over Auphonic + Audacity is paid back in fewer tool switches per episode.
