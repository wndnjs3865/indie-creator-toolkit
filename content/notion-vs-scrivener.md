- **Pick [Notion](https://notion.so)** if you value: solo creators wanting an all-in-one workspace, writers who like database-driven docs.
- **Pick [Scrivener](https://www.literatureandlatte.com/scrivener)** if you value: long-form fiction and screenplay writers.

If you're starting from zero, both have free tiers — try one for 30 days, see if it fits your workflow before committing further.
