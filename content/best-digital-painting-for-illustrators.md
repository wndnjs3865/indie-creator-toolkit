## FAQ

**Is there a truly free option for digital painting software?**
Yes — Krita is free forever.
For everything else, the free tier is enough to validate before paying.

**What's the cheapest paid option that's still serious?**
Procreate at $13/mo.

**Do I need digital painting software at all when starting out?**
Honestly, no. Most illustrator ship their first project with whatever's on their laptop already. Tools matter once friction starts costing you hours per week.
