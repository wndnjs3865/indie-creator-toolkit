## Where Procreate wins

- One-time payment, no subscription
- Best iPad experience

### Procreate downsides

- iPad only
- No layers groups limit on big files

## Where Affinity Photo wins

- Free as of 2025
- Native Mac/Windows + iPad
- Pro feature parity

### Affinity Photo downsides

- Smaller plugin ecosystem than Photoshop
- Canva ownership = future uncertain

## Verdict

- **Pick [Procreate](https://procreate.com)** if you value: iPad-first illustrators.
- **Pick [Affinity Photo](https://affinity.serif.com)** if you value: Photoshop alternative without subscriptions.

If you're starting from zero, the free tier of either lets you ship your first project before you commit. That's the right way to choose.
