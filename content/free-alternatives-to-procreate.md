## When you should just pay $13 for Procreate

If iPad with an Apple Pencil is your primary setup and you'll use it daily, $13 once is genuinely cheap for the best touch-painting experience on the planet. Free alternatives are right when you're not on iPad, you're learning before committing, or you specifically want open-source insurance.

## What to skip

- **Adobe Fresco** — free version exists, but iPad-only and the workflow is locked into Adobe's ecosystem.
- **Sketchbook** — was free, then paid, now in limbo. Not stable enough to recommend.
- **MediBang Paint** — free, fine for first sketches, but Krita's brush engine + active development put it ahead.
- **Concepts** — vector-first, wrong tool for raster painting.
