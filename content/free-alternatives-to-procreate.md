[Procreate](https://procreate.com) costs $13/mo. If that's not in the budget yet, here are free options that get most of the job done.

## Quick comparison

| Tool | License | Platform | Best for |
|---|---|---|---|
| **[Krita](https://krita.org)** | Open source | Mac, Windows, Linux, Android | digital painting on any budget |
| **[Affinity Photo](https://affinity.serif.com)** | Free (proprietary) | Mac, Windows, iPad | Photoshop alternative without subscriptions |

## Each alternative in detail

### Krita

[Krita](https://krita.org) is open source and serves the same workflow as Procreate for most beginners use cases.

**Why it works as a Procreate alternative**

- Open source
- Brush engine is excellent
- Animation timeline included

**Where it falls short of Procreate**

If you need iPad-first illustrators, you'll eventually outgrow this.

### Affinity Photo

[Affinity Photo](https://affinity.serif.com) is free to use and serves the same workflow as Procreate for most beginners use cases.

**Why it works as a Procreate alternative**

- Free as of 2025
- Native Mac/Windows + iPad
- Pro feature parity

**Where it falls short of Procreate**

If you need iPad-first illustrators, you'll eventually outgrow this.

## When you should just pay for Procreate

If you're already making money from your work and Procreate would save you 2+ hours/week, the math says pay for it. Free alternatives are right when you're validating an idea or learning the craft.
