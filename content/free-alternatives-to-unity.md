## When you should just pay for Unity

If you're shipping 3D commercial games at scale, mobile is your primary platform with monetization, or you specifically need the asset store ecosystem — Unity's $25/user/mo (only past $200k/yr revenue) is honest pricing. Free alternatives are right when you want zero cost, open-source insurance, or you're avoiding Unity post-2023 trust issues.

## What to skip

- **Unreal Engine** — incredible tool, wrong audience for indie. The 5% royalty after $1M is fine for AAA-aspiring devs but heavy for solo work.
- **Construct 3** — solid no-code, but the comparison is for engines you can scale into a career.
- **Bevy** — Rust ECS engine, exciting but tooling maturity in 2026 still trails Godot for non-Rust devs.

## Honest disclosure

Both Godot and Defold are free, open source, and earn no commissions. We compare them because they're the right pick for different readers — full stop.
