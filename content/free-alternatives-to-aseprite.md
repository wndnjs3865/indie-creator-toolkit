[Aseprite](https://www.aseprite.org) is $20 for the prebuilt binary — or free if you compile from source. For some readers, neither path fits. Here are the genuinely-free pixel art editors that work in 2026.

## When you should just pay $20 for Aseprite

If pixel art is your job (or you're seriously trying to make it your job), $20 once is genuinely cheap for the genre standard. Free alternatives are right when you can't pay, you specifically want GPL/MIT licensing, or you're learning before committing to the workflow.

## What to skip

- **GIMP** — general raster editor, possible to do pixel art in but the workflow fights you the whole way.
- **Photoshop with pixel-art preset** — overkill subscription for the use case.
- **Online pixel editors (Piskel etc.)** — fine for absolute first sprites, but you outgrow them fast.
